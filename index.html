Equations & Inequalities Mastery
Key Concepts
Master the properties of equality (=) and inequality (<, >). Learn how adding or multiplying both sides affects equations, and understand how negative multiplication/division reverses inequality direction.
Calculation Strategy
  • Write each calculation step in sequence to prevent sign errors
  • Apply the distributive property thoroughly when handling brackets
  • Practice 10 problems daily of the same type
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
Polynomial Operations
Understand Fundamentals
Distinguish between terms and coefficients. Master like-term simplification and polynomial multiplication basics.
Calculation Technique
Write signs prominently to prevent visual errors. Maintain consistent expansion order to build reliable habits.
Practice Method
Train with "round-trip calculations"—expand expressions then factor them back. Mix problems with fractions and negative numbers.

Functions & Graphs
Core Concepts
Master function definitions and substitution. Convert freely between equations, tables, and graphs. Understand the meaning of slope and intercept.
Calculation Tips
  • Be meticulous with parentheses during substitution
  • Pay attention to signs in numerator/denominator when calculating slope
  • Practice 5-minute daily "coordinate calculation speed checks"
Geometry: Lines & Angles
Essential Concepts
Master parallel lines, corresponding/alternate angles, and interior/exterior angle relationships. Recognize basic structures where angles sum to 180° or 360°.
Calculation Strategy
Immediately mark angles and lengths on diagrams. Find hidden angle relationships by adding auxiliary lines.
Practice Method
Solve consecutive angle calculation problems within 10 minutes. Develop the habit of redrawing figures from problem descriptions.
Mastering Triangles
Key Concepts
Master congruence conditions (SSS, SAS, ASA, AAS) and similarity conditions. Apply the Pythagorean theorem and basic trigonometric ratios.
Calculation Focus
Mark all given conditions on the figure without omission. Be careful with units and decimal points when converting lengths and angles.
Practice Approach
Train with "length inference" games after presenting conditions. Maintain trigonometric intuition through right triangle problems.
Circle Geometry
Core Theorems
Understand inscribed angle theorem, tangent properties, and relationships between chords and central angles. Master calculations for sector area and arc length.
Calculation Tips
Prevent errors in angle unit conversion (degrees/radians). Simplify calculations by utilizing perpendicular conditions.
Daily Practice
Quickly solve 5 tangent/chord problems daily. Try multiple solution approaches for the same conditions to build flexibility.
